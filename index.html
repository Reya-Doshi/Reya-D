Reflection of a Student

Sometimes I pause and stare At the quiet stranger in the mirror. I wonder where the laughter went, Where the girl who once sparkled disappeared. There was a time when mornings felt light, When dreams stretched wide like the open sky. Books were not burdens on tired shoulders, And hope never questioned why. Now the nights grow longer than they should, Pages whisper but my heart feels still. The weight of tomorrow sits beside me, A silent test of strength and will. I remember a version of me Who smiled without thinking twice, Who believed the world was gentle And kindness came without a price. Sometimes I miss that simple soul, The one who danced through every day. But somewhere beneath the tired eyes She still refuses to fade away. Because even in quiet battles And in moments when spirits fall, A student learns more than lessons She learns how to rise through it all.

Khushiyon Wali Zindagi

Kabhi kabhi lagta hai zindagi Ek chhoti si kahani hai, Jahan har din ek naya safar Aur har pal ek nayi nishani hai. College ke raaste, doston ki baatein, Hasi jo rukne ka naam na le, Coffee ke cup aur lambe sapne, Jaise waqt bhi saath chale. Kabhi library ki khamoshi, Kabhi canteen ki awaaz, Inhi chhoti chhoti lamhon mein Chhupa hota hai jeene ka raaz. Kabhi kisi sheher ki galiyon mein Khud ko thoda sa kho dete hain, Aur phir kisi muskaan ke saath Zindagi ko dobara pa lete hain. Dil mein pyaar, aankhon mein roshni, Aur sapne aasman se bade, Yeh student wali zindagi hi toh hai Jahan kal ke darr bhi lagte hain thode. Phir bhi hum chal padte hain Haste hue, gaate hue, Kyuki zindagi ka asli maza Isi safar mein milte hue.

Little Things

Happiness is rarely loud, It does not arrive with fireworks in the sky. Sometimes it hides in the quiet corners Of ordinary days passing by. It lives in a warm cup of tea On a morning that feels slow and kind, In sunlight slipping through the window And peace settling in the mind. It waits in small, careless laughter Shared between friends after class, In songs playing through earphones While crowded days slowly pass. It is the message from someone you love, The breeze on a long evening walk, The comfort of knowing someone listens Even when you barely talk. Life is not only grand moments Or dreams that loudly shine. Sometimes the heart learns its greatest joy From the smallest things in time.

Three Hearts, One Home

Love, to me, is not a grand story. It is a small home filled with familiar voices. Three hearts that beat beside mine In the quiet rhythm of everyday life. My mother carries warmth in her hands, The kind that turns ordinary days gentle. In her eyes lives a silent strength That holds the whole world together. My father stands like steady ground, Not always loud with words, But his presence feels like shelter On days when life feels uncertain. And my sister, my little universe of chaos, Half my laughter, half my fights, The one who knows every version of me From childhood dreams to sleepless nights. We are not perfect people, Just four souls learning life together. But in this small circle of love I have found my forever.
